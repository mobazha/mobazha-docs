# Founding Whitepaper Maintenance Contract

- Current public version: `0.1-review`
- Lifecycle: Draft
- Canonical English source: [`content/en/project/whitepaper.md`](../content/en/project/whitepaper.md)
- Maintained Chinese translation: [`content/zh-CN/project/whitepaper.md`](../content/zh-CN/project/whitepaper.md)
- Last reviewed: 2026-07-04

The rendered English page at `/project/whitepaper` is the policy-authoring
source during the first public phase. The Chinese page is a maintained
translation and must not introduce a separate promise.

## Version path

- `0.x-review`: public review drafts; direction, not shipped guarantees.
- `1.0`: first approved founding whitepaper after the publication gates in
  RFC-0001 are satisfied.
- `1.x`: editorial clarification that does not change durable principles.
- `2.0` or later: a material change to trust, economic, governance, or system
  principles that requires a new RFC and explicit supersession.

Every version change records the affected sections, reason, reviewers,
decision record, and relationship to current release scope. Release behavior
continues to be governed by implementation, contracts, capability responses,
tests, and release evidence—not by the whitepaper alone.

## Excluded material

Do not add confidential operating plans, forecasts, customer information,
credentials, incident details, experimental percentages, or claims unsupported
by the public release boundary.
