## Current outcome

The current priority is a trustworthy release-candidate loop: discover effective capabilities, operate or connect a store, publish listings, complete supported checkout and payment paths, manage orders, preserve evidence, recover data, and understand every cost and responsibility.

## Near-term outcomes

- Harden standalone installation, backup, upgrade, recovery, and release provenance.
- Complete coherent buyer and seller journeys across small and large screens.
- Keep hosted, standalone, marketplace, and sovereign compositions on shared contracts rather than product forks.
- Make API, event, webhook, MCP, extension, and Agent behavior discoverable and fail closed.
- Improve store administration, storefront presentation, shipping, identity, accessibility, and internationalization based on validated user needs.
- Publish compatibility and conformance evidence for capabilities claimed across distributions.

## Exploration areas

AI-assisted store setup, Agent-mediated workflows, reusable commerce surfaces, richer marketplace operations, additional payment integrations, and new extension runtimes remain subject to capability, security, economic, legal, and release review. Exploration is not a delivery commitment.
