## What these demos are

Every demo in this gallery proves one promise to one persona by showing the
real product keep it. Demos are recorded from scripted, deterministic runs
against a running Mobazha stack — never mockups, never spliced outcomes — and
each recording keeps its provenance: what was recorded, when, and against
which build.

- Masters are silent 16:9 recordings narrated by on-screen chapter cards, so
  the same footage reads correctly in a social feed, an embedded player, or a
  conference screen.
- Where a demo shows payments, it is recorded on the Mobazha test network and
  says so on screen.
- A demo of capability that is not yet in a general release is labeled
  **Preview** until that capability ships.

## Seller Affiliate Loop (Preview)

*Sellers offer promoters a storefront-wide cut; promoters share one short
link; commissions settle on-chain when order funds release.*

| | |
|---|---|
| Primary persona | Promoter (with seller and buyer supporting) |
| The pain | "My followers buy the gear I review — the brand keeps everything." |
| The payoff | A promoter's earnings page: paid-out totals and a confirmed commission with its on-chain transaction hash. |
| Length | 76 seconds, silent master |
| Recorded | 2026-07-18, on the Mobazha test network |
| Status | Preview — the short-link share surface is in rollout |

What the chapters show:

| # | Role | Chapter |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Seller | Offer promoters a cut — 5% on every attributed order, storefront-wide. |
| 2 | Promoter | See what each sale pays — every item shows your cut before you commit. |
| 3 | Promoter | Grab your link — and a QR — one short link, bound to your wallet. |
| 4 | Buyer | One tap from a post — the referral is saved, shopping stays normal. |
| 5 | Buyer | Buy as usual — escrow-protected checkout, paid in crypto. |
| 6 | Seller | No invoices, no spreadsheets — commissions settle when funds release. |
| 7 | Promoter | Paid on-chain, automatically — confirmed commissions land in your wallet. |
